THIS IS
OUR CHOICE

We own our bodies yet the government believes they have control over them. It should be our choice to either carry a pregnancy to term or abort, not theirs. Additionally, abortions are a right that can prevent unnecessary death due to pregnancy complications.

Let Individuals Control Their Own Choice

The choice has always been ours, but politics are taking our choice away. Our campaign was put into motion when Amanda Eid lost her child in the womb and was denied an abortion in Texas due to laws put in place. The fetus was long dead and risking going septic yet the law still prohibited it. This must be stopped.

It isn't easy

Pregnancy isn't an easy thing for a woman to go through. Through the 9 months, a woman will experience weight gain, increased heart functions, changes in hormones, and much more. It is not an easy life change and can result in health risks if the pregnancy goes wrong.

It can be hard

Sometimes, tragedy strikes and the fetus does not survive. Choosing an abortion for any kind of pregnancy is always going to be a tough choice for the mother, but it should always be a path they can take. Especially when the fetus dies in development which can lead to extreme health risks for the mother.

It can be a risk

Limiting a woman's choice can mean an inevitable demise for many. Sometimes, an abortion is necessary and lawmakers are making it impossible. The ban on abortions is killing people.
